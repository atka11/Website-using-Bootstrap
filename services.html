A Complete Vet Service

With a Tier 3 small animal hospital, as well as two extra small animal practices, Vets & Pets can offer a wide range of services at a choice of locations in Wiltshire.

We are proud to be one of the few remaining independently owned practices that offer our own in-house emergency service, giving clients the peace of mind that whatever the time, their pet will receive top care from Vets & Pets.


A personal service

Client experience is very important to us and we pride ourselves on offering a personal vet service. We also do our very best to ensure you can see the same veterinary surgeon whenever you visit, for optimal continuity of care.

We offer a range of routine surgical procedures and have our own surgical referrals service onsite, so you can relax knowing that you only need Vets & Pets for your pets.


We're here for you and your pets

Please do not hesitate to contact us with any queries or questions about your pet, or the vet service we provide. Call the clinic on 01327 350239 to speak to our reception team or to request a call back from a vet.

We like our clients to be able to make informed choices when choosing the best treatment for their pet so will always provide up to date and relevant information about our vet services.

  • Dental care

  • Nurse clinics

  • Vaccinations
